Cádiz, Spain

In Port: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM · Dinner in Town
☀️ Sunny & Warm
🌡️ High 72°F / Low 62°F
💨 Atlantic breeze
🌊 Sea temp 67°F
⚓ Arrive 8:00 AM Depart 10:00 PM
One of the oldest cities in Europe, Cádiz is a whitewashed gem on a peninsula jutting into the Atlantic. Its golden-sand beaches, narrow Moorish lanes, fish markets, and tapas bars are magical. Note: Your "Explore Like a Local" tour (10 AM–2 PM) is on the WAIT LIST — confirm availability before going. If it doesn't come through, Cádiz is incredibly walkable on your own. The ship departs at 10 PM, so you have a full evening in town — this means a casual dinner ashore.
Excursion
"Explore Like a Local" — 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM (WAIT LISTED). A walking exploration of Cádiz's old town, markets, and hidden spots. Comfortable, casual, and outdoors the entire time.
Outfit — Day
Sightseeing + Walking Tour · Warm & Sunny

A breezy sundress (knee or midi length) in white, pale yellow, or light blue — fitting for Cádiz's whitewashed aesthetic. White leather sneakers or comfortable walking sandals with good arch support (cobblestones!). Small crossbody bag. SPF 50+, sunglasses, and a wide-brim hat. Bring a light cotton cardigan tied around your waist for the evening Atlantic breeze.

Dinner (In Town — Casual)
Dinner in Cádiz · Evening in Town
Ship departs at 10 PM, so you have a relaxed evening to dine in town. Cádiz is famous for its fresh seafood and tapas. Head to the La Viña neighborhood near the beach or the Mercado Central area. Casual-chic attire — your daytime sundress freshened up with a statement earring and a light blazer or wrap is perfect. Comfortable sandals or sneakers since you'll be walking back to the ship afterward.

Tangier, Morocco

In Port: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM · Dinner on Ship
⛅ Warm with some cloud
🌡️ High 70°F / Low 62°F
💨 Moderate breeze
🕌 Dress Modestly
⚓ Arrive 8:00 AM Depart 6:00 PM
Welcome to Africa! Tangier sits at the confluence of the Atlantic and Mediterranean, with a layered history of Phoenicians, Romans, Arabs, and Europeans. The medina is a maze of color, spice, and sound. No organized tours available for this port — explore independently. The Grand Socco square, Kasbah, Medina souk, and the American Legation Museum are highlights. Important: Dress very conservatively in Tangier. Modest dress is respectful and expected in Morocco.
Outfit — Day (Modest Dress Required)
Morocco Exploration — Culturally Appropriate

Lightweight linen trousers (wide-leg or straight) in a neutral color (tan, white, grey) + a loose, breathable long-sleeved blouse or linen tunic that covers your shoulders and arms. Comfortable flat walking shoes or leather sandals with a strap (the medina has uneven cobblestones). A light scarf to drape over your shoulders or head near mosques. A secure zip-top crossbody bag — keep it in front of you in the souk. Skip sleeveless tops, short skirts, or revealing necklines out of respect.

Dinner
Dinner on the Ship
The ship departs at 6 PM, meaning you'll return before dinner. This is a ship dinner — dress smart casual to elegant. A beautiful wrap dress or cocktail-adjacent dress. This is a good night for one of your dressier options since you'll be back early.

Motril (Granada), Spain

In Port: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM · Dinner on Ship
☀️ Sunny & Warm
🌡️ High 70°F / Low 61°F
🌴 Tropical microclimate
💨 Light breeze
⚓ Arrive 9:00 AM Depart 8:00 PM
Motril is the gateway to Granada — home to one of the most stunning palaces in the world, the Alhambra. The area has a unique tropical microclimate (the only place in Europe where sugarcane and avocados grow commercially). Your tour takes you inland through the stunning landscape and local culture.
Excursion
"A Journey Through Taste and Time" — 4 hours. A culinary and cultural exploration of the region, likely involving tastings of local specialties (tropical fruits, olive oil, cured meats). You may be standing, walking, and eating — dress comfortably.
Outfit — Day
Culinary Tour + Warm Coastal Day

A relaxed but put-together look: slim linen trousers or a casual midi skirt + a breathable blouse or lightweight top. Comfortable white sneakers or leather walking sandals. Small crossbody bag. SPF 50+, sunglasses, hat. A light cardigan in your bag for the drive or any air-conditioned venues. This is a food tour, so avoid anything too tight or restrictive — you'll want to enjoy all the tastings!

Dinner
Dinner on the Ship
Departing at 8 PM, you'll be back on board for dinner. Dress elegant for ship dining — a beautiful dress and your favorite jewelry. This is a lovely evening after a food-filled day.

Cartagena, Spain

In Port: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM · Dinner on Ship
☀️ Sunny & Warm
🌡️ High 72°F / Low 62°F
🏛️ Roman ruins & sunshine
💧 Very low rain chance
⚓ Arrive 9:00 AM Depart 6:00 PM
Cartagena is a stunning city of layered history — Carthaginian, Roman, Moorish, and modernist. The Roman Theatre (Teatro Romano) is one of Spain's most important archaeological sites. Your tour covers 5.5 hours, so you'll have a thorough exploration. The city is sunny and very walkable.
Excursion
"The Most of Cartagena" — 5½ hours. A comprehensive guided tour of the city's highlights: Roman ruins, the castle, the MURAM regional archaeology museum, and Modernista architecture. Lots of walking on uneven terrain.
Outfit — Day
5.5-Hour Walking Tour · Hot & Sunny

Comfortable, breathable sundress (midi or knee-length, not too short for climbing into ruins) or linen trousers + a light cotton tee. Wear your best walking sneakers or strapped walking sandals — this tour involves uneven terrain and stairs. Wide-brim hat, SPF 50+, sunglasses. A small backpack is better than a handbag for such a long tour — it keeps your hands free. Bring a refillable water bottle.

Dinner
Sakura @ 8:00 PM · On Board
Dinner at Sakura, Explora I's Pan-Asian restaurant — a stunning culinary experience. Dress elegantly: a silk or satin dress, statement earrings, and a light blazer or cover for the AC. This is a special restaurant deserving of a beautiful outfit. Think sophisticated and refined.

Saint-Tropez, France

In Port: 9:00 AM – 11:30 PM · Dinner in Town
🌤️ Warm & Mostly Sunny
🌡️ High 69°F / Low 55°F
💨 Light breeze
🚴 Cycling Tour Day!
⚓ Arrive 9:00 AM Depart 11:30 PM
Saint-Tropez! One of the world's most glamorous destinations. The old port (Vieux Port), the Place des Lices market, and the stunning Pampelonne beach area await. Your cycling tour runs 4 hours, exploring the town and its surroundings on two wheels. After the tour you have a long afternoon and evening before the late 11:30 PM departure — perfect for exploring the charming village, boutique shopping, or sitting at a harbor café. Dinner is in town.
Excursion
"Cycling Through Saint-Tropez" — 4 hours. A bike tour exploring Saint-Tropez's scenic streets, coastal paths, and the surrounding countryside. Relatively flat but energetic.
Outfit — Morning (Cycling Tour)
4-Hour Bike Tour · Active & Sporty

Athletic leggings or bike shorts + a moisture-wicking, fitted top. A lightweight windproof jacket tied around your waist or packed in a small backpack (the morning can be cool, and you'll want it while coasting). Athletic sneakers or cycling shoes. A small backpack for sunscreen, water, snacks, and your phone. Hair back in a braid or ponytail under a helmet. SPF 50+ — you'll be in full sun.

Outfit — Afternoon & Evening (Saint-Tropez Village)
Village Exploring + Dinner in Town

Change into something that captures Saint-Tropez's legendary chic: a flowy, floral or solid-color midi dress in white, blush, or sun-yellow + strappy flat sandals or espadrilles (cobblestones in the village). A structured rattan or leather bag. Gold jewelry. Light linen blazer for dinner as the evening cools to 55°F. Saint-Tropez rewards those who dress with flair — be inspired by the Riviera glamour but keep it wearable and walkable. This is the port where you can have the most fun with your outfit.

Dinner (In Town — Casual-Chic)
Dinner in Saint-Tropez · Evening in Town
Ship departs at 11:30 PM — you have a long, luxurious evening. Dine at one of Saint-Tropez's famous spots: La Tarte Tropézienne for pastries, or the harbor restaurants for fresh bouillabaisse and rosé. The town is stylish but not stuffy at dinner — your Riviera-chic ensemble is perfect. The floral midi dress with a blazer and espadrilles or flat sandals is ideal.

Monaco / Monte Carlo

In Port: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM · Dinner Ship or Town
☀️ Sunny & Beautiful
🌡️ High 70°F / Low 57°F
💎 Dress to impress
🏎️ French Riviera Glamour
⚓ Arrive 9:00 AM Depart 9:00 PM
The crown jewel of the Riviera. Your excursion takes you to Nice (the grand Promenade des Anglais, the Old Town/Vieux-Nice, and the famous flower market) and Èze (a breathtaking medieval village perched on a cliff with views of the Côte d'Azur). Back in Monaco, explore the Place du Casino, Prince's Palace, and the famous harbor of Monte Carlo. The ship departs at 9 PM — dinner is your choice of ship or town.
Excursion
Nice and Èze — Day excursion. Nice Old Town is flat and walkable; Èze village involves a steep, cobblestone climb. Sturdy shoes are essential. After, return to Monaco for independent exploration of the Principality.
Outfit — Day
Nice + Èze Excursion · Monaco Chic

Monaco and the Riviera demand a step up in polish. Wear a sophisticated linen or cotton midi dress in a classic color — cream, navy, or soft sage — with a fitted blazer or tailored linen jacket. For shoes: leather walking sandals with a slight heel or wedge espadrilles (NOT flat flip-flops for Èze — the climb is steep and slippery). Structured leather or straw tote. Gold jewelry, chic sunglasses, and a silk scarf tied at the neck or on your bag. A hat with a brim. For the Casino area in the evening, keep the blazer on — there is an informal dress code (no shorts, flip-flops, or sportswear).

Dinner
Dinner — Your Choice · Ship or Monaco Town
Ship departs at 9 PM. If dining in Monaco: the town is famously expensive and glamorous — dress your absolute best. A chic cocktail dress, heels or elegant flats, statement jewelry. If dining on the ship: smart elegant as always. Either way, Monaco is the most glamorous port of the trip — dress for it.

Livorno / Florence, Italy

In Port: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM · On Our Own
⛅ Mixed — Chance of Showers
🌡️ High 68°F / Low 53°F
🌂 Bring umbrella!
🎨 Renaissance Art Day
⚓ Arrive 8:00 AM Depart 8:00 PM
Livorno is the port for Florence — about a 1.5-hour drive or train ride away. You're exploring on your own today, which gives you complete flexibility. Florence is one of the most beautiful cities in the world: the Duomo, the Uffizi Gallery, Ponte Vecchio, the Boboli Gardens, and incredible food. Book the Uffizi and Accademia (Michelangelo's David) tickets in advance. Florence in May can have occasional showers — bring your compact umbrella. The ship departs at 8 PM so plan to leave Florence no later than 5:30 PM.
Outfit — Day
Florence Exploration · Possible Rain

Smart-casual and practical: dark-wash slim jeans or tailored trousers (easier to navigate museums) + a beautiful blouse or fitted top. A lightweight packable rain jacket in a neutral color (navy, camel, or olive) is essential today — it's your best defense against Florence's May showers. White leather sneakers or very comfortable walking shoes with cushioned soles (Florence's Pietra Serena stone streets are unforgiving). Compact umbrella in your bag. A structured leather tote or backpack for your belongings. Note: Churches require covered shoulders — bring your light scarf or a cardigan.

Dinner (In Town — Casual/Independent)
Dinner on Our Own — Florence or Back on Ship
If timing allows before departure, try a trattoria near the market area — Mercato Centrale or the Oltrarno neighborhood are excellent. Florentine bistecca (steak), ribollita, and fresh pasta are must-tries. Casual-chic attire — your day outfit is fine for a relaxed Florentine trattoria. If you return to the ship for dinner, dress elegant as usual for ship dining.

The journey ends at Civitavecchia, Rome's port, approximately 1 hour from central Rome. Disembark at 9:30 AM. Comfort meets polish for disembarkation day. Smart casual trousers or dark jeans + a beautiful blouse or fitted top + white leather sneakers. A lightweight blazer for the transfer. A roomy tote or carry-on for your essentials. SPF 50+ and sunglasses for Rome sightseeing. For the Vatican (if visiting): covered shoulders and knees are strictly required — bring your scarf and ensure your trousers cover your knees, or wear a midi skirt/dress.

Return Flight Note
Your return flight is Saturday, May 16 from Rome (FCO) at 9:00 AM, arriving Lexington (LEX) at 5:12 PM. You have May 13, 14, 15 in Rome before your early morning flight. Enjoy La Dolce Vita — aperitivo at sunset, Roman pasta, and long evenings at Piazza Navona!
